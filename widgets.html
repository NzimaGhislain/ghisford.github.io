How the farmarket app works

install the app onto your mobile device.On the first launch,the app will take you to the login page where you will fill in your login details based on whether you are a buyer,farmer or delivery personnel. The deatils will then be verified by the system after wich you can be able to login. if you register as a seller, the details of your produce will be added to the system by an administrator. if you registered as a buyer, you can be able to place your order for the produce by adding to the cart. The app will then use information about the location of the seller and the weight of the items added to determine the quickest available means of delivery
